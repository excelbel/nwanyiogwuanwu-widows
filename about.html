About Great and Ground Initiative

We are committed to restoring dignity, independence, and hope to widows and vulnerable children through empowerment, education, financial support, and community care.

Our Mission

To empower widows and children through holistic support, creating opportunities to thrive in safe and nurturing environments.

Our Vision

A world where widows and children are empowered to live with dignity, independence, and hope for the future.

Our Journey

2020

Launched the first community outreach program serving 200 widows and children across the region.

About the Founder

Dr. Helen Ugbo is a distinguished professional with a strong background in business and accounting. She has devoted her career to public service, entrepreneurship, and the empowerment of vulnerable populations. With a Doctorate in Management from Highstone Global University, Brooklyn, New York, and certification from the Chartered Institute of Management Accounting (CIMAN), Dr. Ugbo brings a wealth of knowledge and leadership to every role she undertakes.

Education

Onyeabo Memorial Primary School Prince Memorial High School Oko Polytechnic (OND and HND, Business Studies) University of Nigeria, Enugu Campus (B.Sc. Accountancy) Chartered Institute of Management Accounting (CIMAN) Highstone Global University, Brooklyn, New York (Doctorate in Management)

Professional Experience

Dr. Ugbo began her career with the National Youth Service Corps at the National Electric Power Authority (NEPA) in Edo State. She later worked as Estate Manager at Pokobros Group, where she gained valuable management experience. As Chief Executive Officer of Akona Real Estate, she established herself as a successful entrepreneur. Her passion for philanthropy led her to found the Nwanyi Ogwuanwu Widows Foundation, an organization dedicated to empowering widows and other vulnerable populations.

Political Career

In addition to her professional and philanthropic work, Dr. Ugbo has been actively involved in politics, having served as former Local Government Chairman of both the All Progressives Congress (APC) and the Young Progressives Party (YPP) where she championed inclusive leadership and grassroots development.

Personal Life

Dr. Ugbo is happily married and a proud mother of four children. She is passionate about service to humanity, showing compassion, and creating lasting impact within her community.

Key Strengths

Leadership: A proven track record of guiding organizations and initiatives with vision and integrity Entrepreneurship: Demonstrated success in real estate and business development Philanthropy: A lifelong commitment to supporting vulnerable groups and driving social change
