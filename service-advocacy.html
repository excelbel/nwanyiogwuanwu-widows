Advocacy

True change happens when voices are raised together. Our Advocacy initiatives focus on raising awareness about the challenges widows face, and lobbying for policy changes that protect their rights.

We partner with community leaders, NGOs, and governments to amplify widows’ voices and create a more just and inclusive society for widows and their children.
