Education Support

Education is one of the most powerful tools for breaking cycles of poverty and rebuilding futures. Through our Education Support program, we provide scholarships, school supplies, mentorship, and ongoing academic support for widows and their children.

We also partner with vocational institutions to create opportunities for widows to learn skills that can sustain their families and restore financial independence. Every child and every widow deserves a chance to learn, grow, and thrive.
