Skills Training

Economic empowerment is key to restoring dignity. Our Skills Training programs provide widows with practical and marketable skills such as tailoring, catering, computer literacy, and small business management.

By equipping widows with these skills, we open pathways for entrepreneurship and employment that lead to sustainable independence for themselves and their children.
